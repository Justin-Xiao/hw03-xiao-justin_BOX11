4.fnlwgt: continuous. 
5.edu_num: continuous. 
6.relation: Wife, Own-child, Husband, Not-in-family, Other-relative, Unmarried. 
7.race: White, Asian-Pac-Islander, Amer-Indian-Eskimo, Other, Black. 
8.sex: Female, Male. 
9.cap-gain: continuous. 
10.cap-loss: continuous. 
11.hours: continuous.
